Mailing list simuaa

We have set up a mailing list for the numerical simulation community, with the aim to share informations, news, job openings, events announcements, etc...

  1. To register to the mailing list simuaa, please send an email to: sympa@services.cnrs.fr with the subject "subscribe simuaa Firstname Lastname"
  2. To unsubscribe to the mailing list simuaa, please send an email to: sympa@services.cnrs.fr with the subject "unsubscribe simuaa"
